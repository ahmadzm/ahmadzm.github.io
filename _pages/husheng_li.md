**Professor Husheng Li** is the principal investigator of the Li Research
Group. He holds appointments in Aeronautics & Astronautics and Electrical &
Computer Engineering at Purdue University. His research spans wireless
communications, cyber‑physical systems, UAV sensing, and control-communication
co-design for autonomous systems.

Dr. Li received his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Princeton University
and B.S./M.S. in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University. Prior to
joining Purdue in 2022, he spent 15 years at the University of Tennessee and
many years in industry, including roles at Qualcomm working on 5G standards.

His work has been supported by the National Science Foundation and other
agencies; he has multiple patents and over 100 peer-reviewed publications.
